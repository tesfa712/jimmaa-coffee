OUR STORY

Jimmaa Coffee brings the authentic taste and culture of Ethiopian coffee to your neighborhood. Named after Jimma, the birthplace of coffee in Ethiopia, we honor the centuries-old tradition of coffee ceremony while providing a modern, welcoming space for our community.

Our beans are sourced directly from Ethiopian farms, ensuring the highest quality and supporting sustainable farming practices. Each cup tells a story of rich heritage and meticulous craftsmanship.

At Jimmaa Coffee, we're more than just a coffee shop - we're a community hub where people connect, work, and experience the true essence of coffee culture.
