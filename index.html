BUSINESS OVERVIEW & VISION

Jimmaa Coffee will be a cultural coffee shop serving the local community and nearby workers. Our shop will provide a warm, welcoming environment with high-quality coffee and a unique Ethiopian-inspired atmosphere.

Drawing inspiration from the birthplace of coffee in Jimma, Ethiopia, we aim to create an authentic experience that celebrates coffee's rich history while providing a modern, comfortable space for our customers.

Our vision extends beyond just serving coffee - we want to create a community hub where people can connect, work, and experience the cultural heritage of coffee's origin.

SPACE & OPERATIONS PLAN

Space Utilization

Jimmaa Coffee will occupy the available 600 sq. ft. retail space, featuring a coffee bar, seating area, and display shelves. The layout is designed for efficient operations and an inviting customer experience.

Coffee Bar & Service

Our coffee bar will be the centerpiece, featuring state-of-the-art equipment for brewing traditional Ethiopian coffee alongside modern espresso beverages.

Seating & Atmosphere

The seating area will accommodate approximately 20-25 guests with a mix of individual tables, communal seating, and comfortable lounge areas.

Retail Display

Display shelves will showcase Ethiopian coffee beans, traditional coffee brewing equipment, and related merchandise for customers to purchase.
